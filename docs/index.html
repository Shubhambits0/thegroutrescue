FAQs

Common Questions Answered

How long does a full shower regrout take?
Most standard shower regrouts take between 2–4 hours depending on the size and condition of the grout. We'll give you a clear time estimate when we quote the job.
How soon can I use my shower after regrouting?
We recommend waiting 24 hours before using your shower after regrouting and silicone work. This allows everything to cure properly and ensures the best long-term result.
Do you do free quotes?
Yes, absolutely. All quotes are 100% free with no obligation. For most jobs we can provide an estimate over the phone or from a few photos. More complex jobs may require a quick on-site visit.
Can you match my existing grout colour?
In most cases yes. We carry a wide range of grout colours and can match or closely match your existing grout. If you want to change the colour entirely, we can discuss your options.
What areas of Melbourne do you service?
We service all Melbourne metropolitan suburbs — inner city, eastern, western, northern and southern suburbs. If you're unsure, just give us a call and we'll confirm we can get to you.
Do you work on weekends?
We work Monday to Saturday. Saturday appointments are available but book out quickly, so it's best to contact us early in the week to secure a weekend slot.
Is your work guaranteed?
Yes. We stand behind every job we do. If you're not satisfied with the result, we'll come back and make it right. We use quality materials designed to last and give you clear aftercare advice.
Can you help with real estate / end of lease jobs?
Absolutely. We work with property managers, landlords and real estate agents regularly. We understand the urgency of end-of-lease and pre-sale jobs and prioritise fast turnaround times.
